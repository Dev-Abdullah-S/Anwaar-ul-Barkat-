Privacy Policy

Anwaar ul Barkat — Last updated: July 2, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Anwaar ul Barkat ("we", "our", "us"). This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Anwaar ul Barkat is a digital book library that allows users to browse and read books directly within the App. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

2. Information We Collect

Anwaar ul Barkat is designed with user privacy in mind. The App requires no user registration or login to browse and read books. As a result, we collect minimal information:

a) Automatically Collected Data

  • Network requests — the App connects to our servers to fetch the book catalogue and book files. Standard server logs may record your device's IP address and request timestamps.

b) Locally Stored Data

  • Cached files — downloaded book files (PDFs) and cover images may be cached on your device for faster access. This data remains entirely on your device and is not transmitted to us.

We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, physical address, precise location, contacts, photos, or any other sensitive personal data from users of the App.

3. How We Use Your Information

The minimal information we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Service delivery — to display the book catalogue and deliver book content to you.
  • App improvement — to maintain and improve the functionality and performance of the App.
  • Security — to protect against misuse and ensure the stability of our services.

4. Data Storage & Security

Book catalogue data is stored on Supabase, a trusted backend-as-a-service platform that uses PostgreSQL databases with encryption at rest and in transit.

Book files and cover images are stored on Cloudflare R2, a secure cloud storage service. Access to these files is controlled through authenticated server-side functions.

All data transmission between the App and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.

5. Third-Party Services

The App relies on the following third-party services to function:

  • Supabase — Database and backend services. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Cloudflare — File storage via Cloudflare R2. Cloudflare Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Services — Required for app distribution on Android. Google Privacy Policy

These third-party services may collect information as described in their respective privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

We may share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers — with the third-party services listed above, solely to provide the App's functionality.
  • Legal requirements — if required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.
  • Safety — to protect the rights, property, or safety of our users or the public.

7. Data Retention

Since we do not collect personal user accounts or personal data, there is no user-specific data retained on our servers. Standard server logs (such as IP addresses) are retained for a limited period for security purposes and then automatically deleted.

Locally cached files on your device can be deleted at any time by clearing the App's data through your device settings or by uninstalling the App.

8. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since the App does not require registration or login and does not collect personal data, it is safe for use by individuals of all ages.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has somehow provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
